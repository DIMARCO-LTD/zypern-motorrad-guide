Contract Formation

Die Darstellung der Produkte und Dienstleistungen auf der Website stellt kein rechtlich bindendes Angebot, sondern eine Aufforderung zur Bestellung (invitatio ad offerendum) dar. The presentation of products and services on the website does not constitute a legally binding offer, but rather an invitation to order (invitatio ad offerendum). By completing the ordering process and clicking the “Buy Now” button, the customer submits a binding offer.

The contract is concluded by the Provider's declaration of acceptance, either by an express order confirmation by email or by the provision of the purchased digital content. The Provider is entitled to reject orders without stating reasons.

To exercise your right of withdrawal, you must notify us (ZypernMoto GmbH, Musterstraße 42, 10115 Berlin, email: widerruf@zypernmoto.de) by means of a clear statement (e.g. a letter sent by post or an email) of your decision to withdraw from this contract.
